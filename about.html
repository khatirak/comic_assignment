About This Interactive Comic

Welcome to Where's Wally the Walrus

This is an interactive comic book that allows you to choose the path of the story. There are 2 paths to choose from, and 2 endings to each path. You get to decide where Wally ends up!

The story was inspired by the original Where's Wally? comic book, but with a modern twist. An attempt to create an interactive story that allows you to choose the path of the story, and where Wally ends up.
